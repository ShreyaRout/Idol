Ambani Family Tree: Meet all the members of ‘The Ambani Family’


Dhirubhai Ambani, the legendary Indian business tycoon, had two sons: Mukesh Ambani and Anil Ambani. Mukesh Ambani is the older son and is currently the Chairman and largest shareholder of Reliance Industries, a conglomerate with interests in petrochemicals, refining, oil, and gas exploration. Under Mukesh's leadership, Reliance Industries has diversified into telecommunications, retail, and digital services, making it one of the largest and most valuable companies in India. Mukesh Ambani is known for his visionary leadership and strategic acumen in growing the business empire. Anil Ambani is the younger son of Dhirubhai Ambani. He is also a prominent figure in the Indian business world, though his career has taken a somewhat different trajectory from that of his brother. Anil Ambani has been involved in sectors such as telecommunications, power generation, and financial services through companies like Reliance Communications and Reliance Power. However, Anil Ambani faced challenges in recent years due to financial difficulties in some of his businesses, leading to significant restructuring and asset sales. Both Mukesh and Anil Ambani have had significant impacts on the business landscape in India and have inherited their father's entrepreneurial spirit. However, their divergent paths and business approaches have made their individual journeys unique.

Mukesh Ambani

Dhirubhai Ambani's eldest son is Mukesh Ambani. Mukesh Ambani is an Indian billionaire businessman and the chairman of Reliance Industries, a conglomerate with interests in petrochemicals, refining, oil, telecommunications, and retail. He is one of the wealthiest individuals in India and globally, known for his vision and leadership in expanding the family business into various sectors. Mukesh Ambani has played a significant role in reshaping India's economy and has been involved in various philanthropic activities as well.

Anil Ambani

Anil Ambani is the younger son of Dhirubhai Ambani, the founder of the Reliance Group. Anil Ambani is an Indian business tycoon who has been involved in various businesses like telecommunications, infrastructure, entertainment, and financial services. Anil Ambani was the chairman of Reliance Communications, a telecommunications company, and the Reliance ADA Group, which had interests in power, infrastructure, and financial services. However, due to financial challenges and debts, Anil Ambani's businesses faced significant difficulties in recent years. As of my last knowledge cutoff in December 2023, Anil Ambani had been dealing with legal battles related to debts owed by his companies. Dhirubhai Ambani and Kokilaben Ambani had four children, namely Mukesh Ambani, Anil Ambani, Nina Kothari, and Deepti Salgaonkar. Here is a brief description of each child:

1. Mukesh Ambani: Born on April 19, 1957, Mukesh Ambani is the eldest son of Dhirubhai and Kokilaben Ambani. He is a successful businessman and the chairman of Reliance Industries, one of India's largest conglomerates. Mukesh Ambani is known for his strategic vision and leadership in expanding the family business across various sectors, including petrochemicals, refining, oil, telecommunications, and retail.

Anil Ambani: Anil Ambani, born on June 4, 1959, is the younger son of Dhirubhai and Kokilaben Ambani. Like his brother Mukesh, Anil was involved in the family business and held key positions in Reliance Group companies. Anil Ambani focused on businesses related to telecommunications, infrastructure, entertainment, and financial services. However, his businesses faced challenges in recent years, leading to financial difficulties and legal issues.

3. Nina Kothari: Nina Kothari is the eldest daughter of Dhirubhai and Kokilaben Ambani. She has maintained a relatively lower profile compared to her brothers Mukesh and Anil Ambani. Nina Kothari's involvement or public ventures are not as extensively documented as those of her brothers.

4. Deepti Salgaonkar: Deepti Salgaonkar is the second daughter of Dhirubhai and Kokilaben Ambani. Similar to Nina Kothari, Deepti Salgaonkar has maintained a private life and has not been actively involved in the public business endeavors of the Ambani family.
